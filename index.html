What is the DMVCC?

Simply, A collective of cheesemongers & shop owners making the DMV one of the foremost areas of cheese knowledge and offering.

We are working together to share knowledge and accumulated experience in the goal of furthering the DMV as a preeminent destination for artisan cheese!
